Books

Tagged and annotated bibliography of related books.

The Tacit Dimension / Michael Polanyi

Polanyi’s compact collection of three lectures provides foundational insight to his theories of tacit knowledge and emergence and their impact on our understanding of the growth and evolution of the human mind.

Polanyi, Michael. The Tacit Dimension. The University of Chicago Press, 2013.

tags: learning methods, personal knowledge, students


For White Folk Who Teach in the Hood ...and the Rest of Y'all Too / Christopher Emdin

Examines the use of “reality pedagogy” as a way of approaching modern education. Emdin subscribes to and promotes the idea of creating a welcoming educational space as a teacher that requires going beyond the typical class-planning routine to foster genuine, culturally conscious bonds with those in your classroom.

Emdin, Christopher. For White Folks Who Teach in the Hood... and the Rest of Y'all Too: Reality Pedagogy and Urban Education. Beacon Press, 2017.

tags: pedagogy, learning methods, teachers, students, high school
